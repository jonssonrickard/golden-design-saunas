## 3. Infrared Saunas
Infrared saunas use invisible infrared heat. While you cannot see it, you can feel it. An infrared sauna heats up more rapidly compared to traditional saunas and has a bit of a different feel to it. These saunas are quite popular in modern times, considering the long existence of saunas, or more specifically, traditional saunas.

## 4. Traditional Saunas
Traditional saunas use a stove, or a heater, which warms up the air around it. These saunas are sometimes called steam saunas because users will apply water to the rock which then produces steam.

## 5. Hybrid Saunas
Hybrid saunas, are well, just as you may have guessed; a combination of both infrared and traditional.
